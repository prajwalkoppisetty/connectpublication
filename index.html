What Is The Use Of Handwritten Sheets?

These notes are asked to be written in loose sheets. Notes of the same topics are collected from different books of different writers. When written in loose sheets, it makes it easy to edit. Those collected notes are edited and re-published in a new title so that there is no copyright claim.

How Do We Get Paid?

There is an option in the application form to choose the payout mode. You can choose NEFT/GOOGLE PAY/PHONEPE/NEFT/UPI/e-wallet such as PAYTM mode of payment to receive your earnings.

Who Will Bear the Charges?

For the time being, Company bears writing material & all stationary. But at the end, these materials, and courier charges will be deducted from your book payment.
